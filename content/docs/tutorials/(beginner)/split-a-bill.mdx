**What you'll build:** the amount each person owes for a shared meal, tip included.

**What you need:** Nothing but this page.

**Why this matters:** it's the fastest way to learn multiplication, division, and
order of operations — the backbone of every spreadsheet.

## Step 1 — Add a tip

A 20% tip on a $80 bill is the bill times `0.20`.

```formula test
=80*0.20 // => 16
```

<TryIt formula="=80*0.20" />

**What just happened:** `*` multiplies. 80 × 0.20 = 16.

## Step 2 — The total with tip

```formula test
=80*1.20 // => 96
```

<TryIt formula="=80*1.20" />

**What just happened:** multiplying by `1.20` adds the 20% in one step.

## Step 3 — Each person's share

Split the $96 total between 4 people.

```formula test
=80*1.20/4 // => 24
```

<TryIt formula="=80*1.20/4" />

**What just happened:** `/` divides. TrueCalc does `*` and `/` left to right.

## Try changing…

- Your own bill amount, tip rate, and number of people.

## You learned

- `*` multiplies, `/` divides.
- Multiplying by `1.20` adds 20% in one step.
